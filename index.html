TEDxLewistown is an event designed to support Central Montana by providing a platform for local individuals to listen, learn, and network with other members of the community who want to make a difference.

Join us for a day of inspiration and challenge, September 6th, 2019.

We are currently in the planning stage of this event. We are determining the venue, and still need to book staff, speakers and volunteers.
